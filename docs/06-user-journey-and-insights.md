### 6.2 Narrative Case – AI Housing Opportunity Agent

#### Helping newcomers in Barcelona secure housing and administrative stability

Newcomers arriving in Barcelona face a fragmented housing market where critical information is often hidden, incomplete, or uncertain.  
This project explores how an AI-powered housing agent could help identify opportunities with a higher probability of success.

---

### 1. Problem

Barcelona attracts thousands of newcomers every year, including immigrants, students, freelancers, and international professionals.

However, finding housing in the city is a complex and stressful process. Information is fragmented across multiple platforms and informal channels, and key details such as **empadronamiento compatibility** are rarely clearly stated.

For newcomers, securing housing is not only about accommodation. It is often the first step toward administrative integration, enabling access to healthcare, legal documentation, and other public services.

As a result, the housing search process becomes highly manual, uncertain, and time-consuming.

---

### 2. Discovery

This case study is based on user discovery and lived experience navigating the housing market.

Several patterns emerged during discovery.

#### Fragmented Information

Housing opportunities are spread across multiple sources:

- Idealista  
- Badi  
- Facebook groups  
- Instagram pages  
- Personal networks  

Users must manually monitor multiple platforms.

---

#### Low Response Rate

Tenants often send **dozens of messages** to landlords before receiving a reply.

In some cases, users may send **50+ messages** before securing a viewing.

---

#### Time Sensitivity

Listings often receive many messages shortly after being published.

Landlords typically review only the first group of inquiries, making **early discovery critical**.

---

#### Administrative Constraints

For many newcomers, housing must allow **empadronamiento** in order to:

- Access healthcare
- Complete residency documentation
- Integrate into the local administrative system

However, this information is rarely clearly stated in listings.

---

#### Trust and Scam Risks

Informal housing channels often contain:

- Incomplete information
- Misleading listings
- Potential scams

Users must manually evaluate trust.

---

### 3. Key Insights

Discovery revealed several important insights.

#### Housing discovery is a probability problem

Users are not just searching for housing — they are searching for **opportunities with a real chance of success**.

---

#### Early access increases response probability

The likelihood of receiving a response decreases significantly as a listing ages.

---

#### Administrative compatibility matters more than property quality

For many newcomers, the decision hierarchy is:

1. Empadronamiento compatibility  
2. Budget  
3. Location  
4. Property characteristics  

---

#### Quality of opportunities is more valuable than quantity

Users often prefer a **small set of curated opportunities** rather than thousands of uncertain listings.

---

### 4. Product Opportunity

Existing platforms focus on listing volume rather than **opportunity quality**.

A product could instead focus on identifying:

- Recent listings  
- Compatible listings  
- Responsive landlords  
- Trustworthy opportunities  

This creates an opportunity for a **housing discovery intelligence layer**.

---

### 5. Proposed Solution

The proposed solution combines two components.

#### AI Housing Agent

An intelligent agent that monitors multiple housing sources and analyzes listings using AI.

#### Housing Opportunity Platform

A simple interface where users create a profile and receive curated housing opportunities.

The system focuses on **surfacing high-probability opportunities** rather than maximizing listing volume.
