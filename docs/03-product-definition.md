## 3. Product Definition

This section defines the product vision, value proposition, and key features of the AI Housing Opportunity Agent.

---

### Product Vision

To simplify housing discovery for newcomers in Barcelona by transforming fragmented housing information into clear, trustworthy, and high-probability opportunities through AI-powered insights.

The **goal** is to reduce the stress, uncertainty, and manual effort required to secure housing and achieve administrative stability in a new city.

---

### Product Description

The AI Housing Opportunity Agent is a platform that helps newcomers in Barcelona discover housing opportunities with a higher probability of success.

Instead of manually browsing multiple housing platforms and sending dozens of unanswered messages, users create a profile describing their housing needs.

An AI agent continuously monitors housing sources, analyzes listings, extracts key signals (such as empadronamiento compatibility, price, and minimum stay), and matches them with the user's profile.

Users receive alerts when relevant opportunities appear, allowing them to contact landlords quickly and increase their chances of securing housing.

---

### Core Value Proposition

The product helps newcomers identify trustworthy housing opportunities faster by reducing information fragmentation and uncertainty in the housing search process.

Instead of browsing hundreds of uncertain listings, users receive curated opportunities that match their needs and have a higher probability of success.

---

### Main Product Features

- Tenant profile creation (budget, location, empadronamiento requirement, move-in date)
- AI-powered listing analysis that extracts key signals from housing descriptions
- Cross-platform housing discovery (monitoring multiple housing sources)
- Opportunity matching based on user profile
- Opportunity ranking based on compatibility and listing recency
- Real-time alerts when relevant opportunities appear
- Opportunity feed showing curated listings with match scores

---

### How the Product Solves the User Problem

The product reduces friction and uncertainty in the housing search process through several key mechanisms:

#### 1. Reduces fragmented search

Instead of manually checking multiple platforms, the AI agent continuously monitors housing sources.

- Aggregates listings from different platforms  
- Eliminates the need to constantly refresh housing websites  

---

#### 2. Extracts hidden information from listings

Many listings contain important details hidden in text descriptions.

The AI agent analyzes listing content and extracts signals such as:

- Empadronamiento compatibility  
- Minimum stay duration  
- Accommodation type  
- Price and location  

---

#### 3. Prioritizes high-probability opportunities

Not all listings have the same chance of success.

The system ranks opportunities based on:

- Empadronamiento compatibility  
- Budget compatibility  
- Listing recency  
- Profile match  

This allows users to focus on opportunities with a higher probability of response.

---

#### 4. Enables faster action

Speed is critical in competitive housing markets.

The platform sends **real-time alerts** when relevant opportunities appear so users can contact landlords quickly.

---

#### 5. Reduces stress and uncertainty

By surfacing clearer opportunities and reducing manual search effort, the platform helps users:

- Avoid sending dozens of unanswered messages  
- Reduce exposure to scams  
- Focus on housing options that match their real needs
